About US

Our journey towards health mix started 10 years ago when we decided to include something healthy and fulfilling in our daily meal. By incorporating this traditional drink in our diet, we noticed a positive change in our health and mood. Especially, when our kids started to like it and consume with satisfaction. This urged us into expanding our domestic success with our fellow citizens. Particularly during the pandemic, our thought process was to bring additional healthy food products that can improve natural immunity. So our focus was not only to bring health mix into market, but other products that are rice in millets, tasty and easy to cook.
